# Brand Positioning

## Primary Description

**Pensieve is a private memory of how you built.**

## Product Category

**Private cognitive telemetry for AI-native builders.**

## Core Promise

**Preserve the thinking behind the work.**

## Alternative Positioning Lines

* Your private record of engineering judgment.
* Remember the decisions that shaped the work.
* The missing memory layer for AI-assisted development.
* See where your judgment changed the outcome.
* Capture the work that never appears in the pull request.
* A private reflection workspace for AI-native builders.
* Your contribution is more than the code.

## One-Sentence Pitch

Pensieve is a local, privacy-first tool that analyzes AI coding conversations and terminal activity to create a private record of the decisions, insights, friction, and human judgment behind a developer's work.

## Short Pitch

AI coding tools are generating more of the implementation, but the developer's most valuable contribution—judgment—is disappearing inside prompt histories and terminal sessions.

Pensieve runs locally, analyzes those interactions, and creates a private weekly record of the decisions, corrections, insights, and ideas that shaped the work.

It is game tape for how you think, built for reflection rather than surveillance.

## Final Vision

In an AI-native workplace, code volume will no longer be a reliable representation of individual contribution.

The most valuable work will increasingly consist of framing problems, setting direction, identifying constraints, correcting machines, and making judgment calls under uncertainty.

Pensieve exists to preserve that work.

It gives builders a private, structured memory of the moments where their thinking mattered.
